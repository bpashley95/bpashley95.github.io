Welcome to Slovenia Travel - Your Gateway to Slovenian Wonders!

Established 30 years ago, Slovenia Travel has been a pioneer in providing unparalleled travel experiences, immersing our clients in the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Slovenia. As a leading travel agency specializing in this hidden gem of Europe, we take pride in curating unforgettable journeys that showcase the diverse landscapes, vibrant cities, and warm hospitality that Slovenia has to offer.

Our Story:

Since our inception three decades ago, Slovenia Travel has been committed to crafting unique and authentic travel experiences. What started as a passion for sharing the wonders of Slovenia with the world has evolved into a renowned travel agency, renowned for its expertise and personalized service.

Why Choose Slovenia Travel:

  1. Expertise: With 30 years of experience, our team boasts an in-depth understanding of Slovenia's hidden gems, ensuring that your journey is filled with the most captivating destinations and enriching activities.
  2. Personalized Service: We believe that every traveler is unique, and so are their preferences. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to tailor each itinerary to suit your individual needs, ensuring a truly personalized and memorable adventure.
  3. Local Insight: As a company deeply rooted in Slovenia, we have cultivated strong relationships with local communities, businesses, and guides. This enables us to provide you with an authentic experience, taking you off the beaten path to discover the heart and soul of the country.
  4. Sustainable Travel: We are committed to responsible tourism, promoting eco-friendly practices and supporting local conservation efforts. By choosing Slovenia Travels, you not only embark on an extraordinary journey but also contribute to the preservation of Slovenia's natural beauty.
  5. Comprehensive Packages: Whether you seek an adrenaline-fueled adventure, a relaxing escape, or a cultural exploration, our diverse range of packages caters to all tastes. From the pristine lakes of Bled to the vibrant streets of Ljubljana, we have the perfect itinerary for you.

Our Mission:

At Slovenia Travel, our mission is to create travel experiences that go beyond the ordinary. We want our clients to not just visit Slovenia but to connect with its soul, forging memories that last a lifetime. Through our commitment to excellence, authenticity, and sustainability, we strive to be the premier choice for anyone seeking an extraordinary journey through Slovenia.
